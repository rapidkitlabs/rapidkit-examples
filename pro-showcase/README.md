## Publishing Rule

A Pro showcase can be public before the paid product is listed for sale, but it must be honest:

- Explain what the free version includes.
- Explain what the Pro product adds.
- Avoid claiming purchase availability until release evidence and customer delivery artifacts pass the Pro gate.
- Link to free scaffolds, waitlist, or product page instead of exposing private source.
